NAIC (National AI Competition)

Criteria: Team of 4 (Category A: Form 3/Year 9 – Form 5/Year 11)

Challenge:
AI Art: Create an A3 artwork using AI tools.
AI Innovation: Propose AI solutions for real-world problems.
AI Technical: Develop AI models using coding skills.

Submission:
AI Art: Final artwork and creative process writeup.
AI Innovation: Writeup on the problem, solution, social impact, and prototype.
AI Technical: Complete codebase with a detailed methodology.

Due Date: Preliminary: 24 May (Online), Final: Presentation at Sunway University

Prize: Cash, certificate, Google office visit, 6 months premium photo editing

Cost: Free
